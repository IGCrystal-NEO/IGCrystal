### 立即需要帮助的信号 / Signs That Need Immediate Help

  *Thoughts or plans of self-harm or harming others nya* 🚨

- **感到完全绝望，认为情况永远不会好转喵** 😔
  *Feeling completely hopeless, believing things will never get better nya* 😔

- **极度焦虑或恐慌发作喵** 😰
  *Extreme anxiety or panic attacks nya* 😰

- **出现幻觉或妄想喵** 🌀
  *Experiencing hallucinations or delusions nya* 🌀

- **严重的酒精或药物滥用喵** ⚠️
  *Severe alcohol or substance abuse nya* ⚠️

### 需要关注的早期信号 / Early Warning Signs to Watch

- **睡眠模式显著改变喵** 😴
  *Significant changes in sleep patterns nya* 😴

- **食欲大幅度变化喵** 🍽️
  *Major changes in appetite nya* 🍽️

- **社交退缩，避免朋友和家人喵** 😞
  *Social withdrawal, avoiding friends and family nya* 😞

- **学习或工作表现明显下降喵** 📉
